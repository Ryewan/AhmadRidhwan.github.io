Taste Like Garbage

Research has confirmed many risks of regularly consuming Coca-Cola and other sugary drinks. These beverages increase blood sugar levels rapidly and affect the pleasure centers of the brain in a similar way as heroin. More recent research adds to the evidence of a link between sugary drinks and diabetes and seems to confirm the adverse effects of these drinks on brain, kidney, and liver activity. However, consuming Coca-Cola and sugar-sweetened beverages in moderation is unlikely to have a severe effect on health.
